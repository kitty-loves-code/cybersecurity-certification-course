## Course Overview

**From Zero to Cybersecurity Certification** is a **20-week, 33+ chapter online program** designed for small and medium-sized businesses (SMEs) and career-minded professionals. The course offers **2 years of access** to:

- Decades of hands-on experience in **technology, digital business, and IT security**  
- Guidance from an international **Chief Information Security Officer (CISO)** and entrepreneur  
- Weekly **live calls with a global community** of learners  
- Structured support to reach **cybersecurity goals**, implement secure processes, and prepare for certification  

This course makes **professional cybersecurity practices accessible** for organizations that typically invest 6- to 7-digit budgets to secure IT infrastructure, data integrity, bank accounts, and reputation.

---

## Who Should Take This Course

This program is ideal for:

- Business decision-makers and SME owners  
- IT professionals and junior cybersecurity experts  
- Teams preparing for **ISO/IEC 27001, SOC 2, CyberTrust Europe**, and other certifications  
- Professionals responsible for **cyber risk management, fraud prevention, and AI security**  

Companies use this course to:

- Understand the **impact of AI on cybersecurity measures**  
- Upgrade their **overall cybersecurity posture**  
- Reduce **cyber and fraud risks** efficiently  
- Impress **clients and partners** with robust security practices  
- Get **certification-ready** for formal audits  
- Train **junior cybersecurity staff or future CISOs**

## Course Structure

- **Duration:** 20 weeks  
- **Chapters:** 33+  
- **Access:** 2 years  
- **Content:** Strategic, organizational, and communication-focused cybersecurity alongside technical fundamentals  

Participants can start themselves or assign a team member as the **first point of contact for cybersecurity**, growing into a **cybersecurity project manager** who can guide the company through security operations, communication processes, and certification preparation.
